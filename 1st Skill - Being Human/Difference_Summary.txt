DIFFERENCE SUMMARY: With BeHuman vs Without BeHuman

1. SENTENCE LENGTH
   - Without: Long, compound sentences
   - With: Short, simple sentences (one idea per sentence)

2. VERB PHRASES
   - Without: "serves as the historical center"
   - With: "is the historical center"

3. -ING TAIL CLAUSES
   - Without: "fleeing Thomas Edison's patent restrictions",
     "producing thousands of films each year",
     "honoring outstanding achievements"
   - With: Restructured into separate sentences
     "They were escaping..."
     "By the 1920s, Hollywood was producing..."

4. SIGNIFICANCE OVERLAY (removed)
   - "dominant force in global cinema"
   - "major landmark"
   - "most iconic landmarks in the United States"

5. COMMONLY KNOWN AS
   - Without: "commonly known as the Oscars"
   - With: "or Oscars"

6. DECLINE FRAMING
   - Without: "experienced significant decline" +
     "faced economic challenges" + "revitalization efforts"
   - With: "went through a period of decline" +
     "New businesses and theaters arrived"

7. FORMAL TRANSITIONS
   - Without: "However,", "originally erected in 1923 as"
   - With: "was put up in 1923. It was"

8. AI VOCABULARY
   - Without: None detected (clean on this front)
   - With: Same (no change needed)

OVERALL: The "With" version is ~30% shorter,
reads more like spoken English, and avoids
framing everything as historically significant.
